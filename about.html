Origin Story

The Origin of H-Thai-ML started all with 2 people selling street food in Las Vegas, NV. Recipes made from tradition passed down from generations of family. An idea to one day bring this amazing food for all to enjoy, with hard work and dedication their dreams became a reality with now 4 stores open, and more to come. Thank you for being a part of a dream to provide delicious food to all.
